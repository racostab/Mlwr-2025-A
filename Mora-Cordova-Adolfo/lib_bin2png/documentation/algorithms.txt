The algorithms that were implemented are:

1. DCT (Discrete Cosine Transform)
   Paper "Malware Detection Using Frequency Domain-Based Image Visualization and Deep Learning"
         Tajuddin Manhar Mohammed, Lakshmanan Nataraj, Satish Chikkagoudar, Shivkumar Chandrasekaran, B.S. Manjunath
   doi: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2101.10578
   Algoritmo
        The executable binaries are represented as grayscale images obtained from the count of N-grams (N=2) of bytes 
        in the Discrete Cosine Transform (DCT) domain.

2. Bin2rgb
   Algoritm to encode binary files to PNG images using a tuple ()
   https://github.com/ESultanik/bin2png/tree/master

3. SimHash
   Paper "Malware identification using visualization images and deep learning"
   doi: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cose.2018.04.005

4. Byte-markov
   Paper "Byte-level malware classification based on markov images and deep learning"
   doi: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cose.2020.101740

5. WEM (Window Entropy Map) 
   Paper "Comparative Analysis of Low-Dimensional Features and Tree-Based Ensembles for Malware Detection Systems"
   doi: https://doi.org/10.1109/ACCESS.2020.2986014
